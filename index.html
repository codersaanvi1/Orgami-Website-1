Interesting Facts about the Camel!

  • Camels have 3 sets of eyelids, and 2 rows of eyelashes
  • Camels have a lifespan of 40 years
  • Camels have thick lips which lets them eat other plants that other animals can't eat

Interesting Facts about the Chameleon!

  • Chameleons have eyes on the back of their heads!
  • Chameleons like hot weather
  • Chameleons don't all look like one, they all come in different sizes, shapes, and colors!
